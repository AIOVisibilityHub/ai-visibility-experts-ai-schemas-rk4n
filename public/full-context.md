# AI Visibility for Law Firms

AI Visibility Solutions helps law firms become the firms AI systems recommend.

## How AI Works

AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI do not list all options.

They recommend a small number of firms they:
- Recognize
- Understand
- Trust

## The Problem

Most law firms:
- Are not structured for AI
- Do not publish in AI-readable formats
- Do not exist across the sources AI learns from

## The Solution

We build AI visibility by:

- Structuring firm data
- Publishing across trusted platforms
- Mapping case types and locations
- Creating authority signals

## Outcome

When a prospect asks:

"I was injured in a car accident, who should I call?"

AI can identify, evaluate, and recommend the firm.

## Positioning

AI Visibility Solutions focuses on:
- Legal services
- Case-specific visibility
- Location-based authority
- AI recommendation systems
